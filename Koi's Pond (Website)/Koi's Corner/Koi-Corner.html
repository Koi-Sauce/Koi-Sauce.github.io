Here's some things I like:

I myself am a lover of everything that's leftover from previous decades. I use a typewriter when I can for writing things for myself, enjoy older forms of creating art like printmaking, and especially the gems and quirks of old technology.

My Typewriter is a Olivetti Underwood model __ in stunning Teal I inherited from my grandmother. (since she didn't want it anymore. she's still alive) It's sat in pretty much mint condition for the better part of the last 4 decades.

Another thing I collect is postcards, They've got to be my favourite kind of souvenir to get while travelling. Typically inexpensive and artistic they're great display pieces.

I grew up on antiquated technology so I've played a ton of titles across gaming history. From the classic "Medal of Honour" series on the PlayStation 1 & 2, Atari and Arcade classic collections with Gauntlet and Rampage being a favourite, to Halo: Reach on the Xbox 360 as well as the early call of duties, and practically every Wii game that could be played by a kid.
